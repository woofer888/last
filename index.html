üéÆ How $LAST Works

$LAST is a simple, high-intensity buy-and-hold game built on Solana. The rules are easy, but the tension is real. The goal is to be the final eligible buyer before the countdown ends.

üü¢ How to Play

To enter the game, you must buy at least 0.5 SOL worth of $LAST tokens. Once you make a qualifying purchase, you become the current "LAST" buyer and the 20-second countdown begins.

If no other eligible buy (minimum 0.5 SOL) happens within the next 20 seconds, you win the jackpot.

If another player makes a qualifying buy before the timer reaches zero, the countdown resets and they become the new "LAST" buyer.

üí∞ Jackpot & Payouts

If the timer reaches zero with no new eligible buys, the current "LAST" buyer automatically wins the jackpot. Payouts are handled automatically on-chain, and once the jackpot is paid, a new round immediately begins.

üéü Eligibility Requirement

To receive the jackpot payout, the wallet you use to play must hold at least 1,000,000 $LAST tokens (0.1% of supply) at the time of payout. This requirement prevents quick in-and-out farming and ensures real participation in the ecosystem.

üîÅ Endless Rounds

After each jackpot is paid, the game resets and a new round starts automatically. Every qualifying buy restarts the 20-second timer, and every round creates a new chance to become the LAST.

The jackpot prize is always 50% of the total jackpot wallet balance at the time of payout. This ensures the jackpot never drains to zero, keeping the game sustainable and allowing it to continue indefinitely.
